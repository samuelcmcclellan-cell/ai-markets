- **IEA** (International Energy Agency) reports and data
- **Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory / DOE** research and estimates

Use these facts as framing inputs, not as clutter. Synthesize them into a clean strategic market map. Do not turn the slide into a dense data dump. Be explicit about uncertainty where needed — if power-demand forecasts vary across sources, present that carefully and avoid false precision.
